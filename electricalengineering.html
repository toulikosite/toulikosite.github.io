Electrical Engineering

Touliko provides engineering design for electrical building systems as well as stand-alone infrastructure improvement. Our experience with design-build construction has provided us with a unique hands-on approach to electrical design which provides practical, cost effective solutions to complex problems encountered in the field. Touliko Electrical Design Services include:

  • Sports and roadway lighting design
  • Solar Systems
  • Lighting control systems
  • Instrumentation and control systems
  • Engineered information transport systems
  • Generation standby and prime parallel operation
  • Standby diesel and gas turbine generation design
  • Cogeneration systems design
  • Power quality design including harmonic evaluation,
    power factor and transient voltage surge suppression designs
  • Medium voltage power distribution design
  • Medium and high voltage substation modifications design
  • Fire alarm and security systems

Touliko provides electrical engineering services to a broad range of clients. Our client base is as diverse as the services we offer. Each client has special needs, and Touliko strives to provide innovative and efficient design solutions to meet or exceed the client’s needs in the following types of projects:

  • Distribution Systems
  • Boiler installations
  • Process Related Systems
  • Cogeneration

Touliko provides consulting services to assist our clients in better managing their energy and energy-related costs and risks through supply-side and demand-side management services. Touliko is an Energy Star Partner. Touliko Energy Services include:

  • Strategic Advisory Services
  • Project Implementation Services
  • Technical Consulting Services
  • Energy Bill Analysis
  • Energy Procurement Services
