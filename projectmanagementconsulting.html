Project Management

We at Touliko recognize our clients’ need to get the most value from their projects, regardless of whether that client is a commercial company or a government organization. That’s why we developed our Program & Project Management services to incorporate innovative approaches that ensure our customers always accomplish best results from all their project-based efforts.

Touliko’s management consulting experts work together with our clients’ project teams and important personnel throughout the course of their projects. This allows us to achieve precise planning, coordination, and execution of all phases of program and project initiatives. As a result, we are able to implement methods that leverage all our clients’ organizational assets at every stage of the solution process, enabling us to deliver projects that meet all strategic objectives and goals.

Touliko is highly motivated in our belief that we should not only meet our clients’ demands but also accomplish results that surpass them. That’s why we’ve worked tirelessly to develop our services to answer the many organizational demands of government agencies and civilian companies around today.

Touliko is able to aid its clients in:

  • Project Management Office (PMO) Planning and Implementation Support
  • Policy and Standards Development and Implementation Support
  • Program and Project Planning Support
  • Project Portfolio Management
  • Project Scope Planning
  • Project Schedule Development
  • Project Cost Estimating and Control Support
  • Human Resource Planning and Management Support
  • Risk Analysis
  • Project Tracking and Reporting Support
  • Program and Project Management Tools Implementation
  • Configuration Management
  • Earned Value Management (EVM) Support
  • Organizational Project Management Training
  • Project Quality Management Planning and Control Support

We are dedicated to ensuring that the organizations we assist operate at their maximum potential at all times and are continuously empowered through our services.
